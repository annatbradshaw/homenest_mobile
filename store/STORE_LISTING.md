## Full Description

### Apple App Store (4000 chars max)

Take control of your home renovation with HomeNest — the all-in-one project management app designed specifically for homeowners and contractors.

**PLAN YOUR RENOVATION**
Break your project into clear stages like Foundation, Electrical, Plumbing, and Finishing. Set start and end dates, track progress, and never lose sight of what's next.

**STAY ON BUDGET**
Log every expense, categorize costs by labor, materials, or permits, and see your spending in real-time. Upload receipt photos directly from your camera or photo library.

**MANAGE TASKS**
Create tasks linked to specific stages. Set priorities, due dates, and track completion. Filter by status to focus on what needs attention now.

**ORGANIZE SUPPLIERS**
Keep all your contractor and supplier contacts in one place. Store phone numbers, emails, specialties, and ratings. Call or email directly from the app.

**TRACK PROGRESS**
Visual progress indicators show exactly where your project stands. See overall completion percentage and identify stages that are running late.

**DARK MODE**
Full dark mode support for comfortable viewing day or night.

**MULTI-LANGUAGE**
Available in English and Polish, with more languages coming soon.

Whether you're renovating a kitchen, building a new home, or managing multiple construction projects, HomeNest keeps everything organized and on track.

Download HomeNest today and transform chaos into clarity.
